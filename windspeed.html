Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Wind speed, like cloudiness, does not seem to be dependent on location. This graph does not show any true trend lines. If anything it shows that there might be an average windspeed between 5 to 10 mph. There might be an arguement to be had that cloudiness and wind speed might have some correlation in them since clouds can mean rain which might mean stronger winds.
